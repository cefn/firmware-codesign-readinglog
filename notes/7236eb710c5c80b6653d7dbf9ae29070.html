Articulating Software Requirements Comic Book Style

Authors

Amanda M. Williams, Thomas A. Alspaugh

Notes

Argue for a specific form of information presentation, which draws on various advantages of disclosure identified in Comic Books (citing McCloud a lot).

Field of Study

Concepts

Quotations

Anton and Potts (among others) have pointed out what is perhaps the central problem of requirements:

"Stakeholders seldom understand their requirements fully at the beginning of a project. They often lack a clear vision of what the system should do and change their minds during development. In general, stakeholders only become more sure about what they want when they see a system that does not exhibit the necessary features."
