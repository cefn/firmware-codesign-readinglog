Aesthetic principles for instructional design

Authors

Notes

Relates to the pedagogical aspects of CDPF. Principles which could be used in the 'on-ramp' phase of workshop design, to ensure effective introduction to the concepts.

Field of Study

Concepts

Quotations

"Aesthetic experiences are those that are immersive, infused with meaning, and felt as coherent and complete"

"Recent years have seen a surge of interest in reclaiming the idea that instructional design (ID) is indeed a design discipline and more than just science or just technology...In the spirit of this view of ID as design, this article offers principles intended to contribute to developing the aesthetics of instructional design (Parrish 2005). By broadening their concerns beyond immediate learning outcomes and considering all the qualities of designed experiences, instructional designers can create designs that have deep and lasting impacts for learners."
