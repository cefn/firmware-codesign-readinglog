Cultural Probes

Authors

Bill Gaver, Tony Dunne, Elena Pacenti

Notes

The design of boundary objects for bricolage has some strong resonances with the use of Cultural Probes as an open-ended, expressive and materials-mediated form of communication exchange as part of a design process. However, we differ with "we didn't want the groups to constrain our designs unduly", as this is exactly what we want - to be constrained.
