Design Research: a revolution-waiting-to-happen

Authors

Kees Dorst

Notes

A critique of design research, especially prescription for design processes. Contrasts design research and design practice and tries to understand the distance between them. Urges that design processes may need to match content, personalities and context.
They add 'naive' to the sequence of increasing expertise offered by Dreyfus; novice, advanced beginner, competent, expert, master, visionary.

Tags

Relevance

Positions design research as the study and innovation of design practice.
