Notes

All the usual suspects in Boundary Objects terms are referenced, including that Henderson paper again, which I can't lay my hands on. He looks into the way boundary objects relate to each other and have intertwined meanings, by treating them in the same way as texts might be treated, through discourse analysis. Definitely worth a read.
