Eliciting Self-Explanations Improves Understanding

Tags

Relevance

Provides pedagogical evidence that activities which promote the act of explaining something improves your understanding of it. Relevant for the pedagogical aspect of firmware co-design workshops.

Quotations

"Learning involves the integration of new information into existing knowledge. Generating explanations to oneself (self-explaining) facilitates that integration process."
