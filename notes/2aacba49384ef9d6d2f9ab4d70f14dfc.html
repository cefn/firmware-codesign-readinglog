Goal-Framing and Breakdowns in the Design of Boundary-Spanning Information Systems

Authors

Susan Gasson

Year

Notes

A convergence of the use of 'bricolage' and that of Boundary-spanning, within design teams. They are looking at the design of information systems to mesh with organisations, and use this to justify the perspective they are more complex than the design of a regular "technology artifact".
