Carm Down

Authors

Amanda Overend and Elizabeth Stokoe

Notes

Magazine Article on Elizabeth Stokoe's CARM Method

First heard about this technique through Radio 4 'Life Scientific' programme. Uses replay of actual recorded conversations to lead people through more authentic role-play scenarios. Strong relationship with leading paraglider pilots through logged flights as scenario generation and engagement strategy.
