Benefits of Co-design in Service Design Projects

Authors

Marc Steen, Menno Manschot, Nicole de Koning

Relevance

Already found before Facemooc, but provided as part of the Facemooc course reading materials.

Notes

I have a suspicion that the (mainstream field) of Service Design could have a lot in common with Firmware from the point of view of having difficulties materialising the field of work. That is to say, the holistic asynchronous flow of events which combine together to deliver a service are equally hard to concretise, although we would expect users to be able to describe parts accurately, drawing on their common sense, in a way which is perhaps impossible with firmware, because humans can be deployed to deliver the service.
