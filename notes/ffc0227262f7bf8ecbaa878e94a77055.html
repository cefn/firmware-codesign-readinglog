Boundary Objects: Their Role in Articulating the Task at Hand and Making Information Relevant to It

Authors

Ernesto G. Arias and Gerhard Fischer

Notes

They describe specifically the issues of boundary objects for users to program computers, contrasting the situation view adopted by users and the system view which must be adopted to program them. They mention Domain Oriented Design Environments (referencing Fischer). Important to read.
