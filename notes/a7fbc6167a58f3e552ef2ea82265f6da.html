Special Boundary Roles in the Innovation Process

Authors

Michael L. Tushman

Year

Notes

Credited for introduction of boundary spanner as a key area of the innovation process. This treatment covers principally how boundaries of organisations can be transcended by participants who have a role of conduit, but the concept has been broadened and nested, when adopted more widely by the discipline.
