The Design with Intent Method: A design tool for influencing user behaviour

Notes

This defines a set of stimulating cards intended for design teams in the 'concept generation stage'. This paper uses the example of a "Human Technology" interaction problem - 'reducing the likelihood of a customer leaving his or her card in an automatic teller machine'.
