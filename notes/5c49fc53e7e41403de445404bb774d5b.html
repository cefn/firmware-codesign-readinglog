Notes

Incredibly close to my own focus (at least when considering using workshops without prepared boundary objects). Looking at elective representations by users would be a fantastic way to identify underlying regularities just as David Hendry does.

Really clear taxonomy of icon components within diagrams, and covers modelling cases such as UML. It is a very cool paper and well worth reading a few times.
