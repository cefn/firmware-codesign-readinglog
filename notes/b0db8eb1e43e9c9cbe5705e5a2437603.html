Participatory design needs participatory communication: New tools for sharing user insights in the product innovation process

Quotations

"The experiences of real people concerning real product use situations are of great value for designers especially during the earlier phases of the design
process. Such information needs be practically useful during idea generation. We believe that this can be realised if the tool supports communication through three qualities: by enhancing empathy, by providing inspiration, and by supporting engagement. We have developed a tool to make the receivers of the information, the designers, participative in the act of communicating. In this way they become co-creators and co-owners of the information, which may result in higher degrees of acceptance and use."
