A visual guide (short dictionary) of reference terms for interaction designers. Tesler's law of conservation of complexity is extremely relevant in describing how behaviour is mapped to affordances within a firmware-controlled product.

Design patterns and pattern language (credited to architect Christopher Alexander) are possibly relevant to modelling firmware.

Flowcharts should be assessed specifically as a model (perhaps the most naive suggestion of a suitable boundary object).

Cognitive load theory worth examining...(Miller's Magic Number)
