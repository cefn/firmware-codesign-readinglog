Notes

A very long (PhD thesis?),  not yet read in full. Skipped to section on Facilitating Co-Design, in preparation for Manteigas. Discusses card-based design games for facilitation, to combine both catalysing ideas and documenting them. Can leave some voices unheard, only serve some constituencies (e.g. designers). 'Incident cards' and dice.

Concepts

Particularisation- defining and explicating all objects in a roleplay, Presencing - emphasising the present time in roleplay. Personalisation - increasing engagement by incorporating their own objects/elements. [credited to Seland]. Three categories of artefacts - verbal (written and spoken instructions, invitations, questions), institutional (values and norms of context) and material (props and physical context).
