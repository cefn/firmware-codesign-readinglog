Making Sense of Sensing Systems: Five Questions for Designers and Researchers

Authors

Victoria Bellotti, Maribeth Back, W. Keith Edwards, Rebecca E. Grinter, Austin Henderson, Cristina Lopes

Notes

They explore 'sensing systems' as examples of devices without GUI, but WITH behaviour, from an interaction design point of view. A focus is dialogue-controlled devices. Open questions are around what state the system is in, and how it would know your intent toward it, and you would know it's intent too.
