On the Usability of OO Representations

Authors

R. Agarwal, P. De, A. Sinha and M. Tanniru

Notes

Discuss the transformation from functional (process-oriented) and the allegedly more 'data-oriented' Object paradigm in programming languages and development strategies, and discuss how the approaches were considered more or less usable for tasks with different demands.

Field of Study
