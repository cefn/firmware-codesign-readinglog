What is 'Critical' about Critical Design?

Authors

Jeffrey Bardzell, Shaowen Bardzell

Notes

They discuss 'critical design' as introduced by Dunne and Raby, but try to set it in the context of a history of critical thought. Critical design is described as a design practice which subverts the status quo, in other words, channels the work of the designer against global capitalism.

Is Chindogu critical design? Especially given its stated intent never to be sold.
